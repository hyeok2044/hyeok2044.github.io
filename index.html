Welcome to KP Ocean Inc.


At KP Ocean Inc., we adhere to the highest international standards, aligning with the ISO 9001:2015 and MLC 2006 frameworks. Our commitment extends to full compliance with the regulatory and legal requisites set forth by national and international maritime authorities.

1. Crew Excellence

Our dedicated crew members strictly adhere to the STCW requirements, ensuring the highest standards in maritime operations.


2. Quality Across the Board:

From recruitment and embarkation to disembarkation and vacation processes, we continuously strive for excellence, aiming to enhance the satisfaction of our esteemed ship owners and principals.


3. Trust Through Fair Crew Management:

We maintain a high re-employment rate by fostering trust among our seafarers. Our commitment to fair crew management is the cornerstone of this trust.


4. Crew Management System

Our crew management program, inclusive of advanced accounting reporting, boasts updated functionalities. This enables us to provide swift and accurate information, irrespective of location, and facilitates seamless information sharing with our valued customers.




At KP Ocean Inc., our pursuit of excellence in maritime services is unwavering. Join us on a journey where quality meets trust, setting new standards in the maritime industry.
