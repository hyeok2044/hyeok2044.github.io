Welcome to KP Ocean Inc.

KP Ocean Inc. is a rapidly growing company with a variety of ship types. As such, opportunities for promotion are open to you, and we will manage your CAREER and provide a quick path to promotion to captain and chief engineer.

Why Join KP Ocean Inc.?

• Variety of ship types

• Many opportunities for promotion and career development
(From Rating to Officer, Master, C/E)

• Free internet for all fleets

• Rejoining bonus

• Quick and friendly staff response

• No delay of salary

• Cash advance


Application and approval procedures

• Make your resume, then email recruiting@kp-ocn.com) or use our following “send Your Resume” tool.

• The Crew Manager will contact you for an interview via mobile phone or Facebook video call.

• If approved, submit the original documents and undergo a medical test. (Master, C/E, C/O, 1AE may require a principal interview)

• Our designated medical clinic : MICAH MEDICAL.

• Additional training (if required by principals)

• In-house training and dispatch.
